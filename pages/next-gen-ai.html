Course Description

Master the intersection of human creativity and AI precision in this comprehensive bootcamp. You'll learn to harness the power of a modern AI developer stack—Gemini, Mokka, and Cursor—to build, deploy, and scale robust applications.

The course covers the practical implementation of "Vibe Coding," transitioning from natural language prompts to functional prototypes, and finally to production-ready code. You will discover how to leverage AI's efficiency and safety guarantees to automate critical development tasks, preparing you for real-world challenges in the Nordic and Cortex ecosystems.
